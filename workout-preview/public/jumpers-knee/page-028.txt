 
 
 
 
 
JUMPER’S KNEE PROTOCOL 
WORKOUT 3 
 
LANDING 
 
Bilateral Trap Bar/DB Land.  3 sets 5 reps. 
 
 
 
Try to increase the load in this session. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
JUMPING 
 
Standing Box Jump (50% jump).  4 sets 10 reps. 
Pick a box you can comfortably jump onto while landing in 60- to 90-
degrees of knee flexion.  From a standing position, explode up onto the 
box.  Step down, reset, and repeat. 
 
 
ACCEL/DECEL 
Tempo Intervals (70% speed). 10 sets 60 yards.  
Same as previous workout with 10 
sets instead of 8. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
COD  
 
180-Degree 5-10-5 (70% speed). 5 sets ea. way 2 cuts. 
Set up three cones spaced 5 yards 
apart in a box.  Start at the middle 
cone, run towards an outside cone, 
cut, run through the middle cone 
and to the other outside cone, cut, 
and run back through the middle 
cone.  Go at 70%.  For the next set, 
start by going the other direction.  
Then repeat.  Make sure you can 
cut confidently off both sides.
