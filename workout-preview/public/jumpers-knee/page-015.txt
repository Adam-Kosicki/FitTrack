2) KNEE-DOMINANT SQUAT  
 
Pick a squat variation that you tolerate well and can progressively 
add load to over time.  If you find that you really compensate to load 
the hips more than the knees, you can elevate the heels.  Think of 
dropping your pelvis straight down and bringing it straight up.  This 
will load the knees, which is what you want out of a squat.  Control 
down 3 seconds and come up for 3 seconds.  Do a couple warmup 
sets until the weight is heavy enough.  You should have only 2-3 reps 
left in the tank when the set is done.  Week 1 is 5 reps.  Week 2 is 4 
reps.  Week 3 is 3 reps. 3 minutes rest between sets.
