 
 
 
 
 
JUMPER’S KNEE PROTOCOL 
WORKOUT 1 
 
 
LANDING 
 
Bilateral Trap Bar/DB Land.  3 sets 5 reps. 
Stand tall, drop down to a position of around 
60- to 90-degrees of knee flexion and hold for 
2-seconds.  Stand back up and repeat.  You 
should get even bending through the ankles, 
knees, and hips.  
 
 
 
 
JUMPING 
 
Band-Assisted Jump (50% jump).  4 sets 20 reps. 
Grab onto bands or something that will unload your 
bodyweight to a degree.  Squat down to about 60- to 
90-degrees of knee flexion and do a 50% effort jump.  
Jump continuously for 20 reps. 
 
 
 
 
 
ACCEL/DECEL 
Tempo Intervals (70% speed).  6 sets 60 yards. 
Set up four cones spaced 20 yards 
apart.  Start at the first and slowly 
accelerate to 70% speed, you 
should be at that speed by the 
time you hit the second cone.  
Hold that speed until the third 
cone and then slowly decelerate 
to a stop by the fourth cone. 
 
 
COD  
 
45-Degree Zig Zag (70% speed). 5 sets ea. way 3 cuts. 
Set up 5 cones spaced 5 
yards apart at a 45-degree 
angle.  Run through the 
pattern going 70% speed.  
Make sure you can cut 
confidently off both sides.  
Do equal sets going both 
directions.
