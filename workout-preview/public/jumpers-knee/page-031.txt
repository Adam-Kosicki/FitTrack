 
 
 
 
 
JUMPER’S KNEE PROTOCOL 
WORKOUT 6 
 
LANDING 
 
DB Split Land.  3 sets 5 ea. 
Try to increase the load this session. 
 
 
 
 
 
JUMPING 
 
Continuous Approach Jump (50% jump).  4 sets 5 ea. 
Get in an area with open space.  Just like the approach box jumps last 
session, take the same step forward, plant, jump, land, then repeat.  You 
will be moving forward down a path.  Do all reps with one plant, turn 
around, and do the other plant going back.  If you are a single-leg jumper, 
you can do this variation as a single-leg jump instead of double-leg. 
 
ACCEL/DECEL 
Flying Sprints (90-100% speed).  5 sets 20 accel, 10 hold, 30 decel. 
Same as previous workout with 5 sets 
instead of 4. 
 
 
 
 
 
COD  
 
90-Degree Box (90% speed). 4 sets ea. way 3 cuts. 
Same as workout 2.  Intensity has 
changed to 90% instead of 70% 
and sets are 1 less each way.
