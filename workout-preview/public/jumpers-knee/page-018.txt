DAY 3 (HIP HINGE AND STEP UP): 
 
1) ACHILLES HOPPING PROGRESSION  
 
Same progression as covered in day 1.  Move through the progression as pain and function 
allow. 
 
2) HIP HINGE  
 
Pick a hip hinge variation that you tolerate well and can progressively add load to over time.  
Keep a slight knee bend the entire set and hinge back with the hips.  You should feel a good 
stretch in the glutes and hamstrings on the way down. Same tempo, sets, reps, rest, as day 1 
squats and split squats.
