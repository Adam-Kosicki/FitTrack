 
 
 
 
 
JUMPER’S KNEE PROTOCOL 
4) SINGLE-LEG STAND     
Only one option. 
- 
Single-leg Stand.  Stand on the tripod of your foot, bring the opposite leg up, 
hold.  You will probably get some instability at the foot as it quivers.  This is okay, just try 
to always come back to standing on the tripod of the foot.  Once 3-minutes straight 
becomes too easy, you can hold weight in the hands and/or bring the raised leg higher 
in the air.
