 
 
 
 
 
JUMPER’S KNEE PROTOCOL 
WORKOUT 4 
 
LANDING 
 
DB Split Land.  3 sets 5 ea. 
Grab two dumbbells.  From a standing position, quickly drop down and 
land in a split stance.  Hold for 2-seconds.  Stand back up and repeat on 
the other side. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
JUMPING 
 
Continuous Squat Jump (50% jump).  4 sets 10 reps. 
From a standing position, jump up at 50% effort, land in around 60- to 90-
degrees of knee flexion with good bend at the knee/ankle/hip, jump up 
again and repeat for 10 reps. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
ACCEL/DECEL 
Flying Sprints (90-100% speed).  3 sets 20 accel, 10 hold, 30 decel. 
Set up four cones.  The first two, 
spaced 20 yards apart.  The 
third spaced 10 yards from the 
second and the fourth spaced 
30 yards from the third.  You 
will use the first 20 yards to 
build up to a 90-100% sprint, 
hold it for 10 yards, then 
decelerate for the next 30 yards to the last cone. 
 
COD  
 
180-Degree 10-20-10 (70% speed). 5 sets ea. way 2 cuts. 
The same as last session’s 5-10-
5, but the cones are now spaced 
10 yards apart instead of 5.
