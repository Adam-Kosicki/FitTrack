 
 
 
 
 
JUMPER’S KNEE PROTOCOL 
 
STAGE 4 
Return to Sport 
There is no weekly workout for this phase, just suggestions. 
 
If you made the ending of your Stage 3 Store-And-Release program replicate the demands of 
sport, you should be good to go.  When returning to sport: 
 
- 
Keep the same weekly sequence (Day 1 Store-And-Release, Day 2 Isometrics, Day 
3 Lifting) but replace your Store-And-Release day with the sport practice/competition. 
 
- 
If that’s not possible, try to manage sport loads so there is no more than 3 high-
intensity training/competition days in a week (a principle of elite sport in their first year 
of return from patellar tendinopathy). 
 
- 
At least twice per week, maintain Stage 2 Lifting exercises (e.g., squats, hip 
hinges, step ups, split squats). 
 
- 
Continue Stage 1 Isometric exercises as needed (e.g., before or after training, on 
a day off from sport). 
 
- 
For the rest of your athletic career, keep tabs on next day pain.  Take immediate 
action to decrease training volume/intensity if pain responses increase.
