 
 
 
 
 
JUMPER’S KNEE PROTOCOL 
WORKOUT 5 
 
LANDING 
 
DB Split Land.  3 sets 5 ea. 
Try to increase the load this session. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
JUMPING 
 
Approach Box Jump (50% jump).  4 sets 5 ea. 
Pick a box you can comfortably jump onto while landing in around 60- to 
90-degrees of knee flexion.  From a standing position, take one step 
forward, plant, and explode up onto the box.  Step down, go back to the 
start position, and switch the approach style to push off the opposite leg, 
plant, then jump.  Repeat for 5 reps each side. 
 
ACCEL/DECEL

4 sets instead of 3.
