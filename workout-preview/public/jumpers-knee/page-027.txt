 
 
 
 
 
JUMPER’S KNEE PROTOCOL 
WORKOUT 2 
 
LANDING 
 
Bilateral Trap Bar/DB Land.  3 sets 5 reps. 
 
 
 
Try to increase the load in this session. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
JUMPING 
 
Seated Box Jump (50% jump).  4 sets 10 reps. 
Pick a 12- to 18-inch box to sit on with another box in front that is slightly 
higher.  From a seated position, explode up onto the box.  Land in 60- to 
90-degrees of knee flexion.  Step down, reset, and repeat. 
 
ACCEL/DECEL 
Tempo Intervals (70% speed). 8 sets 60 yards.  
Same as previous workout with 8 
sets instead of 6. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
COD  
 
90-Degree Box (70% speed). 5 sets ea. way 3 cuts. 
Set up four cones spaced 5 yards 
apart in a box.  Run through the 
pattern going 70% speed.  Make 
sure you can cut confidently off 
both sides.  Do equal sets going 
both directions.
