 
 
 
 
 
JUMPER’S KNEE PROTOCOL 
WORKOUT 7 
 
LANDING 
 
Staggered Depth Drop.  3 sets 5 ea. 
Find something to drop off.  Land in a 
staggered position with most of the 
weight on the front leg, at  60- to 90-
degrees of knee flexion.  Hold for 2-
seconds, repeat for 5 reps on each side. 
 
 
 
 
JUMPING 
 
Band-Assisted Jump (100% jump).  4 sets 10 reps. 
 
 
 
Same as workout 1 but now at 100%.  Continuous. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
ACCEL/DECEL 
Accel/Decel (70% speed).  6 sets 10 accel, 5 decel. 
Set up three cones.  The first two 
spaced 10 yards apart and the third 
spaced 5 yards from the second.  Start 
at the first cone, accelerate as quickly 
as possible to 70% speed.  Once you hit 
the second cone, start decelerating so 
you can come to a stop by the third 
cone.  You can vary start and stop 
positions with each rep. 
 
 
COD  
 
180-Degree 5-10-5 (90% speed). 4 sets ea. way 2 cuts. 
Same as workout 3.  Intensity has 
changed to 90% instead of 70% 
and sets are 1 less each way.
