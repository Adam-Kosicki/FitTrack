 
 
 
 
 
JUMPER’S KNEE PROTOCOL 
DAY 4 (ISOMETRICS + CALVES/HIPS): 
 
1) QUADRICEPS ISOMETRIC  
 
Same as covered before. 
 
 
2) SOLEUS  
 
Do a bent knee calf raise on a machine or with 
a weight held on the knee.  One leg at a time.  
3 sets of 20 each side.  Just control the reps so 
the soleus muscle experiences fatigue towards 
the end of the set.  Rest 2 minutes between 
sets. 
 
 
3) GASTROCNEMIUS  
 
Do a straight knee calf raise on a machine or with weight.  
Hold onto something for balance.  One leg at a time.  3 
sets of 10 each side.  Controlled tempo, making sure the 
gastroc gets fatigued.  Rest 2 minutes between sets. 
 
 
 
4) BENCH ADDUCTOR/ABDUCTOR  
One knee on the bench, 25 reps each adductor raise.  Switch to both legs up on the bench, 25 
reps each abductor raise.  4 sets.  Rest as needed between sets.
