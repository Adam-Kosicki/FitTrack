WORKOUT 10 
 
LANDING 
 
Bilateral Jump to Unilateral Land.  3 sets 5 ea. 
Stand there, jump up off two legs, and land on one leg, holding for 2-
seconds.  Make sure there is an even bend at the ankle, knee, and hip.  
Start with low jumps until comfortable.  5 reps each side.
